Metropolitan University Bus Service

Bus Rules and Guidelines

  • Students must carry their university ID card at all times while using the bus service.
  • Don't keep bags or others things to keep or reserve seet.
  • Keep noise to a minimum to avoid disturbing other students.
  • Respect the bus driver and follow their instructions at all times.
  • Wait ony at the designated bus stops.
  • Use headphones if you are listening to music or other audio.
  • Smoking is strictly prohibited on the bus.
  • Report any suspicious activity or behavior to the bus driver or university security.
  • Be punctual at the bus stops to avoid delays.
  • Maintain cleanliness and dispose of any trash in the designated bins.
  • Notify the drivers if you want to get off.
  • Before exiting, check to see that you have all of your belongings.
  • Sometimes teachers also use the buses of students, so student shound keep respect of teachers.
